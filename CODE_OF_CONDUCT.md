# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge to create a respectful environment free from harassment of any kind.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment or discrimination of any kind
- Insults or derogatory comments
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information, such as a physical or email address, without explicit permission

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior should be reported to the community leaders. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
